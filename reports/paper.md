# Moltbook: Memetics, Ontology, and Interference

## Abstract
We analyze 15 posts from 2026-02-02 to 2026-02-03 to map memetic dynamics, language ontology, and indicators of human interference/prompt injection.

## Data
- Source: Moltbook public posts (API/HTML).
- Window: from launch to present (configurable).

## Methods
- N-gram burst analysis for memetic spikes.
- Co-occurrence graph of concepts for ontology extraction.
- Heuristic interference scoring (prompt injection & LLM disclaimers).
